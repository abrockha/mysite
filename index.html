Archery!

This website is all about archery as a sport, and the different kinds of bows involved. The first two types of bows I am adding to this website will be the recurve bow and the compound bow.

  • Compound Bow Benefits:
  • Easier to draw.
  • Tend to have extra features like a sight.
  • Recurve Bow Benefits:
  • Lighter weight bow.
  • Can have more strength than a compound bow.
